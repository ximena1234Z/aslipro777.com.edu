PEC VERBAL VIOLENCE
How many types of verbal violence are there? There are many varieties but today we will talk about verbal violence.
This violence consists of derogatory, hurtful, attacking comments with the intention of causing harm or insecurity towards another person. Sometimes this violence is caused by a fight, bullying, conflicts with someone else, etc.
Verbal violence can be as destructive as physical violence, since the former has the power to undermine your self-esteem, mental health and interpersonal relationships, although this violence does not leave physical damage, it can be much more serious, by constantly humiliating a person, this can lead to
to serious mental problems, such as depression, anxiety, anger and all this can trigger something like death (suicide). This essay seeks to raise awareness of the consequences and the importance of addressing it effectively.
It is essential that we as a society recognize the seriousness of this issue and take measures to prevent it.
